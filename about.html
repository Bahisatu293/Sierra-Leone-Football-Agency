Our History: Empowering Sierra Leonean Talent

Founded in 2015, Check Agency Sierra Leone emerged from a passion to nurture and elevate the immense football talent within our nation. We recognized the need for a dedicated organization to bridge the gap between local potential and global opportunities.

Over the past decade, we have diligently worked to identify, train, and successfully secure placements for many promising athletes. Our journey began with a small team and a big dream, focusing on community engagement and grassroots development.

Today, we pride ourselves on a robust network of scouts, coaches, and international partners, facilitating pathways for our players in leagues across Europe, Asia, and Africa. We believe in holistic development, ensuring our athletes are well-rounded individuals ready for success both on and off the pitch.
